Payment Plan for The Forest Living by Majid Al Futtaim

At The Forest Living by Majid Al Futtaim, we understand that purchasing a home is a significant financial commitment. That's why we offer a flexible and convenient payment plan designed to make your dream of owning a luxurious residence in Tilal Al Ghaf a reality. Our payment plan is structured to accommodate a variety of financial situations, ensuring a smooth and stress-free process from reservation to move-in.

Initial Deposit: To secure your preferred residence at The Forest Living, an initial deposit is required. This deposit is a percentage of the total purchase price and demonstrates your commitment to purchasing the property. The exact amount of the initial deposit will vary based on the type and size of the home you select. This initial step ensures that your chosen unit is reserved exclusively for you.

Flexible Installments: Following the initial deposit, the payment plan includes flexible installments spread out over the construction period. These installments are aligned with key construction milestones, providing a clear and transparent timeline for your payments. This approach not only makes it easier for you to manage your finances but also allows you to see the progress of your new home.

Milestone Payments: Throughout the construction of The Forest Living, payments are scheduled at specific milestones. These milestone payments are tied to significant phases of the project, such as the completion of the foundation, structural framework, and interior finishing. This structured approach ensures that your investment aligns with the development progress, offering peace of mind and confidence in the timely delivery of your residence.

Final Payment: Upon the completion of your home, the final payment will be due. This final installment covers the remaining balance of the purchase price and is required before you take possession of your new residence. Our sales team will assist you throughout this process, ensuring that all necessary documentation is completed and that the transition to your new home is seamless and efficient.

Mortgage Financing Options: For those seeking additional financial support, Majid Al Futtaim has established partnerships with leading banks and financial institutions to offer competitive mortgage financing options. Our team is available to guide you through the mortgage application process, helping you secure the best financing solution tailored to your needs. This ensures that you have access to flexible payment terms and competitive interest rates.

Personalized Assistance: We are committed to providing personalized support throughout your home-buying journey. Our dedicated sales and customer service teams are here to answer any questions you may have about the payment plan, offer guidance on financial matters, and ensure that your experience with The Forest Living is positive and rewarding. From initial inquiry to final handover, we are with you every step of the way.
